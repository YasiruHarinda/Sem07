Format:

The students will play the roles of interviewers and the interviewee.
Use the following books as guides for your preparation:
Cracking the Coding Interview (CtCi) - 6th Edition (link - 54MB)
Programming Interview Exposed (PIE) - 4th Edition (link - 16MB) / 3rd Edition (link - 7MB)
Use the following sites to find new/better/interesting questions in your specific area.
www.careercup.com
www.glassdoor.com --> Check for questions from different companies in your area of expertise.
www.hackerrank.com
Read the introductory chapters for interview preparation (Technical and behavioural).
Read and prepare to ask/answer questions from the following (or similar) lists as well
150 Typical Interview Questions
All the groups will cycle through the subject areas as we go forward. Apart from the interviewers, the interviewees must learn the relevant material. Therefore, you all are encouraged to go through & learn both books.
Please bring copies of your CVs/Resumes, and any other relevant documents/certificates for the interviews.
